BUILD YOUR OWN CRYPTOFARM IN SPACE

Metacrypto Universe is the first NFT / Play-to-Earn (NFT / P2E) video game based on cryptomining, with which you can enter to the cryptocurrencies world and their finances, being the owner of your own NFT mining farm.

AN ENTIRE METAUNIVERSE TO WORK ON

You must decide on which planet in the universe to mine according to its characteristics and temperatures, obtaining rewards according to the effectiveness of the mining.

EARN OUR TOKEN WITH YOUR MINING OPERATIONS

The $MTCU will be used as a governance token, with which you can perform different operations and manage your own assets within Metacrypto Universe.
